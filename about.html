Meet Our Team

Passionate travel experts dedicated to creating unforgettable experiences

Alex Rivera

Lead Travel Consultant & Founder

Hello! I'm Alex, the founder and lead travel consultant at Tripnesia Travel. With over 15 years of experience in the travel industry and having visited more than 60 countries across 6 continents, I've dedicated my career to helping others discover the magic of exploration.

My journey began as a solo backpacker in Southeast Asia, where I fell in love with the transformative power of travel. Since then, I've worked with luxury resorts, adventure tour operators, and cultural exchange programs, gaining expertise in crafting personalized travel experiences that go beyond the ordinary.

At Tripnesia Travel, we believe that every trip should tell a story. Whether you're seeking relaxation on pristine beaches, adventure in remote wilderness, or cultural immersion in historic cities, I'm here to make your travel dreams a reality.
